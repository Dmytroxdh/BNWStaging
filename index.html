    "We build too many walls and not enough bridges"

    -Sir Isaac Newton

    Bridges Not Walls (BNW) is an agency that represents a small number of hand-picked international charities. Working with start-ups to multi-nationals, BNW provides world-class marketing, brand design, campaigns, communication and income generation consultancy. BNW also connects the charities it represents with corporate partners, who have pledged their commitment to ESG, CSR and SDGs.

    With a background in Security Risk and Global Intelligence, combined with working for some of the world’s most high profile brands and NGOs, the BNW team is on-hand to deliver impactful strategic solutions.

    Take a look at our partner charities:

    LiberOcto

    An education, sustainable development and human rights organization enabling and empowering local communities to become self-sufficient.

    Chupies

    A global social enterprise that provides work and sustainable, free and reusable feminine hygiene products to women and girls in remote communities.

    Global Outreach Doctors (GoDocs)

    GoDocs provides emergency response doctors and healthcare professionals in high-conflict war zones.

    Green Reef

    A research and education led environmental conservation organisation focused on protecting the Belize barrier reef from degradation.

    AWO International

    a welfare association for humanitarian aid and development cooperation.

    ONE

    An organisation that strives to improve the wellbeing of children in refugee camps by building architecturally designed inspirational play and education spaces
